News & Event From The Institute

You can now continue your studies at any Polytechnic or Direct Entry into 200L on completion of the National Diploma Programme

National Diploma Graduates of the Institute can proceed to any Polytechnic of their choice, remain in the Institute for their Higher National Diploma or apply for direct entry into a university, after successful completion of their course in the Institute

Screening Exercise for Full-Time National Innovation Diploma (2019/2020 Academic Sesssion)

All interested applicants who scored a minimum of 100 in the 2019 UTME are hereby invited to apply for admission. Qualified candidates are required to complete the institution's application form by clicking on THIS LINK on or before Friday, 1st of December, 2019. Upload your passport photograph and ensure you print your application form and screening slip after registration.

Having completed the registration process, candidates are to attend the screening exercise from Monday the 2nd of September, 2019 to Friday the 31st of February, 2020 with the original of their credentials and the following documents - Original & Photocopy of the UTME notification of result slip, Original & Photocopy of DOTS's screening on-line registration form, Original & Photocopy of O'level result(s). For more information call: 08154413298 or 08061364881. Or visit us at 52, Ijemo-Agbadu road, Ake (behind Centenary hall), Abeokuta, Ogun State.
